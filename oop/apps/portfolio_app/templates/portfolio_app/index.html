Why Optimized Options Portfolio (OOP)?


Optimized Options Portfolio (OOP) is a tool for managing a short option portfolio. Shorting options is a high-risk way of playing the stock market and requires care.

The key objectives for managing a short option portfolio are to minimize risk while generating income through widely varying market conditions.

With a proprietary set of rules for opening, closing, and rolling options, OOP provides:

  • Timeline Charts

  • An Easy Interface for Updating Portfolios

  • Clear Trading Suggestions
